Life in Melbourne

Settling into a new city, one walk at a time.

There's a particular rhythm to Melbourne — the trams, the coffee, the laneways. It takes a while to learn how a city moves, and even longer for it to start feeling like home.

"Walking is the best possible exercise. Habituate yourself to walk very far."

Slowly, the streets stop feeling foreign. They start feeling like mine.
