Research

Mixed Reality

Enabling natural interaction between virtual objects and panoramic real-world environments — depth-based occlusion, contact, and collision in 360° scenes captured with RGB-D.

360° Imaging Occlusion RGB-D LiDAR

Computer Vision

6DoF pose estimation from single RGB images in uncontrolled environments, focal length estimation, and 3D scene understanding for robotics and AR.

6DoF Pose Depth Est. CAD Retrieval

Novel View Synthesis

Generating intermediate views for smooth free-roaming navigation between panoramic nodes — moving through captured scenes rather than teleporting between fixed viewpoints.

NeRF View Synthesis Navigation
360° RGB-D Capture
Depth Fusion
